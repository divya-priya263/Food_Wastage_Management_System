Business Loans

The business loan meaning is a loan product offered to business owners who have a running company but require external funds for operations. The investment covers expenses such as employee salaries, rent, buying equipment, or expanding the business to other cities.

Types of Business Loans

  • Term Business Loans: Straightforward short-term loans with a tenure of 1-5 years, based on business credit history.
  • Working Capital Loans: Short-term loans to fulfill short-term liabilities like rent or salaries.
  • Commercial Business Loans: Immediate capital up to Rs 50 lakh for businesses with high turnover.
  • Startup Loans: Loans for startups without collateral, offering repayment flexibility.
  • Equipment Financing: Capital for buying equipment or machinery.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Established enterprises operating for more than six months.
  • Minimum turnover of Rs 90,000 in the last three months.
  • Business not blacklisted or in negative location lists.
  • Charitable organizations, NGOs, and trusts are ineligible.

Documents Required

  • KYC documents, PAN card, and Aadhar card.
  • Last 6-12 months' bank statements.
  • Proof of business registration, GST registration.
  • Additional documents for credit assessment.
