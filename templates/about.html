About Us

Our innovative food waste management system aims to minimize food waste and repurpose it effectively. We offer a platform for individuals to donate food, making it accessible to those in need. Currently, we try to collaborate with over 150 banquets to collect surplus food, which we then distribute to communities facing food insecurity.By addressing food waste, we also tackle hunger—an urgent global issue. We firmly believe that nutritious food should nourish people, not end up in landfills. This initiative is brought to you by:
