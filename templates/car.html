Features and Benefits of Car Loan

  • Get financing for purchasing new and used cars.
  • The financing can go up to 85%-90% of the on-road price of the car.
  • The loan tenure can range from one year up to seven years.
  • The loan amount can be up to three times the annual income of the applicant.
  • Some lenders offer instant financing facilities for cars.
  • The car purchased through financing will be held as collateral until the loan is repaid.
  • The repayment structure most commonly followed for a car loan is equated monthly instalments (EMI).

Factors Affecting Car Loan

Credit score: Your lender will enquire about your credit score to understand your creditworthiness.
Debt-to-income ratio: Lenders assess your income and commitments to see if the new car loan fits your budget.
Down payment: A down payment reduces the loan amount and total interest paid.
Age of vehicle: The age of the vehicle matters in deciding the interest rate for used car loans.

What You Should Know

  • Do your research: Research rates and fees offered by multiple banks.
  • Provide complete documentation: Ensure all documents are valid and ready.
  • Choose the repayment tenure wisely: Calculate the EMI and decide on a comfortable tenure.
  • Consider the total cost of owning the car: Factor in all fees, insurance, and other costs.
  • Check for offers and discounts: Look out for festival offers or special discounts.

How to Apply?

Car loans can be availed through different modes:

  • Directly visit the bank branch.
  • Apply online through your bank’s portal.
  • Get financing through the car dealer.
