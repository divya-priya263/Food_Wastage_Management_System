Buying a House

Buying a house is one of the biggest dreams come true for most people and an extravagant affair altogether. Imparting life to such a dream requires a lot of effort from the buyersâ€™ end and the best one can do to accommodate the home in their budget is through a home loan.

A home loan can be opted to buy a new house/flat or a plot of land where you construct the house, and even for renovation, extension, and repairs to an existing house.

Types of Home Loans

  • Home Loan: The most common type of home loan availed to purchase a house.
  • Home Construction Loan: Financing to construct a house on owned land.
  • Home Extension Loan: Financing for extending an existing house.
  • Home Improvement Loan: For renovating or repairing the house.
  • Home Loan Balance Transfer: Transfer the home loan to another lender for better rates.
  • Composite Home Loan: Financing for land purchase and construction.

Benefits of Taking a Home Loan

Tax benefits on interest and principal repayments.
Lower interest rates compared to other loan types.
Due diligence of property by the bank.
Long repayment tenure (25-30 years).
No prepayment penalty on floating-rate loans.
Balance transfer facility for better interest rates.

Home Loan Interest Rates

The average home loan interest rates are from 6.5% to 12.00% as of March 2021, varying from lender to lender based on multiple factors.

Eligibility Criteria

Banks assess the following factors:

  • Credit history (preferably a score of 750+).
  • Age.
  • Employment type.
  • Minimum annual salary.
  • Collateral security.
  • Assets and liabilities.
  • Residency status.

Documents Required

The following documents are necessary:

  • Identity and residence proof.
  • Last 6 months bank statements.
  • Processing fee cheque.
  • Title documents (if applicable).
